About

COVID-19 - The Pandemic have uniquely challenged the world affecting various effects of which the hard hit is to the economy. We intend to combat and welcome individuals to help & respond to this outbreak by “SHARING”

The outpouring of solidarity and support sparked by this SHARE challenge has been phenomenal

Together we can and we will succeed over this pandemic situation and showcase the unity of humanity

Our Mission

Simply to provide bare minimum necessary food items to the needy on the brink of starvation and pay respect to the frontline warriors

Interested donor may select any of the listed item of desired quantity to pay and order according to respective comfort and willingness on any online e-commerce portal and send it to the address mentioned below, we would SHARE your PRAYERS and BLESSINGS to the needy in our proximity abiding the LOCKDOWN rules and SOCIAL DISTANCING.

Those who intend to share food items in nearby location (Bhubaneswar) once intimated would try to arrange for a pick up and deliver appropriately.

Donors are requested to share a receipt of the ordered item with respective picture to be showcased on the website.
